Challenge: Design a Band

Challenge Information

Design thinking is when you try solving a problem by using empathy towards the people. The reason of why we are studying this is because when you are making a product you have to know how people feel about it, you have to get their ideas in order for your product to be successful. We also study this because you have to know what the people feel.

In this challenge, we used the DEEP design process to design a band. The first step of the DEEP process is discover. In the discover step we got to see the many things that people liked. In the empathize step we tried to feel what our peers thought or felt by asking them questions on what types of fonts they liked what colors they liked. In the Experiment step we put all the ideas and thoughts from the peers that we asked questions from and we tried to make something out of it or atleast get ideas from them to make our own product. In the Produce step we are done with everything and we see our finished product of what we got from the ideas of our peers. A major piece of the challenge was to create an album cover for the band.
